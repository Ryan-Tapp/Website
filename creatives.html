Things I Like

There really is no limit to the range of things I like. From video games like Dark Souls, where I've endured hours of frustration for moments of triumph, to sports like swimming with its early morning practices that left me smelling of chlorine for a decade while growing up. My appreciation spans so many areas that deciding what to highlight here was one of the toughest parts of building this website. But for now, I've narrowed down what I'll share to two relatable passions: Music and Film & TV. The following lists won't be static and will be something I'll continually update to reflect new favourites and discoveries. There are so many incredible songs, movies, albums, and shows that didn't make the cut, but that's not a reflection on their quality; rather, it's a showing of the amazing breadth of media available. And please do remember, I never claimed to have great taste, so proceed with caution.
